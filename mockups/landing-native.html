Respectful contrast

BuddyPress and bbPress proved communities belong in WordPress.

AssemblyPress carries that idea into modern WordPress: fewer fragments, more native surfaces, and a community model built around blocks, DataViews, and the Site Editor.

One native system

Every community surface belongs where WordPress already works.

  • Members shape identity through profiles, directories, roles, and fields that feel native to the site.
  • Conversations live in forums, spaces, topics, replies, and archives that themes can compose with blocks.
  • Community teams manage members, spaces, reports, and moderation through modern DataViews surfaces.

Build the community you own.

AssemblyPress gives WordPress teams one native layer for identity, discussion, moderation, and community pages composed in the Site Editor.
